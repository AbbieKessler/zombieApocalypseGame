Reinforce

You utilize scraps of wood in your basement to strengthen the door and board up windows. While it effectively keeps the undead out, you have great difficulty getting in and out of the house. The food supply is dwindling and you are getting desperate. Your fluffy companion, Jinx, has been surviving alongside you. While the thought horrifies you, Jinx is taking up too many resources and could be a possible source of food. Waiting too long could mean death for both of you.

Do you choose to resort to eating Jinx? Do you choose to leave to find new shelter?
