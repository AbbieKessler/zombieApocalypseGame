Stay

You chose to stay in your small house. The constant screams and the smell of death is overwhelming. The dead are becoming smarter; they broke down your neighbor’s door and turned them all. Your flimsy door can not withstand an attack, but you are also concerned about food. It’s been a week since the undead took over and the few cans of vegetables and soup will not last much longer.

Do you choose to

stay and reinforce your house?

Do you choose to

venture out to find food?
